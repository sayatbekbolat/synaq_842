📋 Instructions

  1. Download or Print: Click the buttons above to save the QR codes
  2. For Testing: Display them on another phone/screen to scan
  3. For Production: Print on A4 paper and laminate for weather protection
  4. Mount Location:
    • START: At the base of Health Stairs entrance
    • FINISH: At the top of the stairs exit point
  5. Height: Eye level (5-6 feet / 150-180cm) for easy scanning

🧪 Quick Test

To test without QR codes, open browser console and run:

localStorage.setItem('synaq_active_attempt', JSON.stringify({
  attemptId: 'test-' + Date.now(),
  userId: 'test-user',
  startTime: Date.now(),
  status: 'started'
}));

Then visit: http://localhost:3000/climbing
